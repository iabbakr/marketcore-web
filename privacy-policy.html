Marketcore Technologies Ltd ("Marketcore", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website at marketcore.com and our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Marketcore services, you may provide us with the following information:

  • Contact information: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Account information: Shop name, shop number, state, city, and local area (for dealers)
  • Identity verification (KYC): National Identification Number (NIN) and Bank Verification Number (BVN) — for dealer accounts only
  • Device information submitted for checks: IMEI numbers and serial numbers entered during device checks
  • Stolen device reports: Device details, description of theft, your contact information
  • Payment information: Email address for payment processing (card details are handled solely by Paystack and never stored by Marketcore)
  • Communications: Messages sent through our contact form or support channels

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and general location (country/city level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Paystack (payment processor) — transaction status and reference numbers only
  • Nigerian government verification services — for NIN and BVN validation (dealer accounts)
  • Firebase Authentication (for dealer mobile app accounts)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service provision: To process device checks and return results to you
  • Report management: To add stolen device reports to our database and notify the community
  • Payment processing: To verify payments via Paystack before releasing device status results
  • Dealer account management: To create, verify, and manage dealer accounts on our mobile app
  • Identity verification: To verify NIN and BVN for dealer KYC compliance (mobile app only)
  • Legal compliance: To comply with Nigerian law, respond to legal requests, and prevent fraud
  • Communication: To send payment receipts, device check results, and important service notifications
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity, false reports, and abuse of our platform
  • Service improvement: To analyse usage patterns and improve our product

We do not use your personal data for advertising purposes or sell it to third-party marketers.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: With Paystack (payment processing), Google Firebase (database and authentication), and Cloudinary (media storage) — all bound by data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by Nigerian law, court order, or government authority — including the Nigerian Police Force (NPF) in connection with theft reports
  • Fraud prevention: To protect against fraudulent activity, including sharing banned NIN/BVN records across our platform to prevent re-registration
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction
  • With your consent: In any other circumstance where you have explicitly authorised us to share your information

Stolen device reports, once submitted, become part of our searchable public database. Reporter contact information is not publicly visible but may be shared with law enforcement upon request.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted to and from our platform is encrypted using TLS/SSL (256-bit encryption)
  • Sensitive data (NIN, BVN) is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • We undergo regular security reviews and penetration testing
  • Payment card data is never processed or stored by Marketcore — all payments are handled by PCI-DSS compliant Paystack
  • Mobile app accounts are secured with Firebase Authentication, 6-digit PIN, and optional biometric verification

While we take every reasonable measure, no system is completely infallible. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@marketcore.com.

5. Data Retention

  • Device check records: Retained for 3 years for fraud prevention and legal reference
  • Stolen device reports: Retained indefinitely as part of our active database (unless legally ordered to remove)
  • Dealer account data: Retained for the lifetime of the account plus 5 years after closure, as required by Nigerian financial regulations
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Nigerian tax law
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for 1 year, then deleted

6. Your Rights

Subject to Nigerian law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Right to restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your personal data
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@marketcore.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Please note: stolen device reports, once verified and added to our public database, cannot be removed at the reporter's request alone — only upon a valid court order.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (session management, security)
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors use our website (anonymised data only)
  • Preference cookies: To remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Payment Information

All payments on Marketcore are processed by Paystack, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you make a payment:

  • Your card details are entered directly on Paystack's secure servers — Marketcore never sees or stores your card number, CVV, or expiry date
  • We receive only a payment reference number and confirmation of success or failure
  • Your email address is shared with Paystack to generate a payment receipt
  • Paystack's own privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data

9. Children's Privacy

Marketcore services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will notify you by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending an email notification to registered dealer accounts
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of our services after the effective date of any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact:

  • Email: privacy@marketcore.ng
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@marketcore.com
  • Address: Marketcore Technologies Ltd, Lagos, Nigeria
