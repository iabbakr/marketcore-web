Our Story

Built by Dealers,
Trusted by Everyone

Marketcore was born out of real frustration — the Nigerian electronics market needed a solution for theft, fraud, and mistrust. We built one.

Our Mission

Making Every Phone Transaction Safe in Nigeria

Every day, thousands of Nigerians buy second-hand smartphones without knowing whether the device they're purchasing is stolen, flagged, or clean. This exposes innocent buyers to legal risk and financial loss.

Marketcore was founded to change this reality. By connecting Nigeria's verified electronics dealers into a single, trusted network — and making that network's data accessible to every citizen — we are building the infrastructure for a safer, more transparent device marketplace.

Our platform is proudly Nigerian-built, legally backed, and driven by a community of KYC-verified professionals who care about the integrity of the electronics market.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Trust & Transparency

Every record in our database is submitted by a KYC-verified dealer. No anonymous reports. No guessing. Every data point is accountable.

Community First

Marketcore is built on the collective power of Nigerian electronics dealers. Our strength comes from our community — verified, local, and real.

Speed & Simplicity

Checking a phone should take seconds, not hours. We obsess over making our product fast, intuitive, and accessible to everyone in Nigeria.

Legal Accountability

All dealer transactions, KYC data, and reports operate within Nigerian law. We partner with legal professionals to ensure our platform is fully compliant.

Dealer Empowerment

Beyond verification, we give dealers the tools to grow their reputation, build trust scores, and conduct business safely through our mobile app.

Pan-Nigeria Coverage

From Computer Village in Lagos to Onitsha Main Market to Kano's Sabon Gari — Marketcore is built for every corner of Nigeria.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2025

The Problem Identified

Our founders — all active electronics dealers — witness firsthand how stolen device fraud costs Nigerian buyers and dealers millions annually. Research begins.

2026

Platform Built

The Marketcore mobile app and backend infrastructure are developed. Initial network of 50 verified dealers onboarded in Lagos for beta testing.

2026

National Expansion

Dealer network grows to 500+ verified members across 12 states. First 10,000 device checks completed. Strategic partnerships with Nigerian legal firms established.

2026

Web Platform Launch

Marketcore.com launches — bringing phone verification to every Nigerian, not just dealers. Anyone can check a phone or report theft for a small one-time fee.

Future

API & Warranty Checks

Marketcore API launches for banks, insurance companies, and e-commerce platforms to integrate real-time device status checks into their services.
